Error Mitigation Module — NISQ Era Noise Compensation
Zero-Noise Extrapolation (ZNE)
Runs circuit at multiple noise levels, extrapolates to zero-noise limit. Applied to all VQE energy estimates. Reduces effective error by ~60%.
Pauli Twirling
Converts arbitrary gate noise into depolarizing noise. Makes error model more predictable and easier to characterize. Applied before ZNE.
ML Error Correction
AI model trained on noise signatures of each backend. Applies post-hoc correction to energy output. Hardware-specific calibration.
